Change lives today

This generation of young youth are facing some of the biggest challenges increased child poverty, high unemployment and worsening mental health as well as physical health. They need the support and encouragement of their youth club now to navigate the challenges ahead.

When you donate, you are making sure all young people have access to much needed support in places where they feel safe, hold trusted relationships and where they choose to go - their youth club. Big or small, your support will give young people a source of hope.

It will help fund young Youth's engaging programmes for young people in awareness programs, conducting campaigns, Mental-Health, Employability, Social Action and Outdoor Learning. Free of charge, we give young people the opportunities to learn crucial life-skills, improve well-being and create lasting friendships.
