Memberships We Offer

We offer three different membership palns according to engaement of people in our club and their roles. The Membership plans are as following;

Lifetime Membership

Active members who played a role to establish this club are honoured with this membership. Any new member who wishes to be in this category must serve the club for atleast of 10 years actively.

Five Year Membership

Any active members who serve as a member of club for at least 2 years are eligible for this membership.

One Year

New person who wants to be a member of club and has a true spirit to serve the nation is welcomed by the club.
